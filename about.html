Dropp Overview

Dropp is an innovative dropshipping system tailored specifically for hostellers at NIT Warangal. This system facilitates the seamless delivery of items ordered from e-commerce sites directly to students' rooms in various hostels.

How Dropp Functions

Dropp employs a simple system where the student enters the details of the package they have ordered on our portal, and delivering it to them from college main gate would be our task.

Real-Time Tracking

Upon placing an order, students can track their delivery in real-time through the website, which provides with regular status updates about the package status.

Convenience

This system addresses the common logistics challenges faced by students, significantly reducing the burden on them.
